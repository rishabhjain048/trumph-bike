REBORN CHOPAR

T450 CROME

Motorcyclists returning from WWII bought stock motorcycles and customized them by removing unnecessary parts and by cutting or “chopping” the fenders and other parts to make the parts and motorcycle lighter and faster. Eventually, modified motorcycles wit chopped parts began to be referred to as “choppers”.
